## Portfolio Items (3 Case Studies)

### Portfolio Item 1: Invoice/Receipt Data Extractor for Accounting Firms

**Challenge:**
An accounting firm was spending 40–80 hours per month manually typing invoice data from 500+ PDF invoices into their accounting system. The manual entry had a 3–5% error rate, leading to reconciliation issues.

**Solution:**
I built an AI-powered invoice extractor that reads PDF invoices and scanned receipts, automatically extracting invoice numbers, dates, vendor details, line items, subtotals, tax, and totals. The tool handles batches of 500+ invoices and outputs clean CSV data ready for QuickBooks import.

**Results:**
- 500 invoices processed in ~67 minutes (from 40–80 hours manually)
- <1% error rate (from 3–5% manually)
- $41,000+ annual savings for a single firm
- Pays for itself in the first month

**Technologies used:** Python, PDF parsing, OCR, AI agents, CSV/JSON export

---

### Portfolio Item 2: Lead Enrichment Tool for B2B Sales Teams

**Challenge:**
A sales team of 5 SDRs was spending 30% of their working hours manually researching company prospects — visiting websites, checking LinkedIn, looking up Crunchbase. Over 500 hours per month of lost selling time.

**Solution:**
I built a batch lead enrichment tool that takes raw company names and automatically researches each one across the web — extracting website URL, company description, industry, LinkedIn profile, location, and estimated company size. The output is a clean CSV ready for CRM import (HubSpot, Salesforce).

**Results:**
- 5 seconds per company (vs. 5–10 minutes manually)
- 2,000 companies processed in ~3 hours (vs. 400+ hours manually)
- $7,415+ monthly savings for a team of 5 SDRs
- CRM-ready output with zero cleanup

**Technologies used:** Python, web search, data extraction, CSV processing, workflow automation

### Portfolio Item 3: SEO Blog Content Engine for Marketing Agencies

**Challenge:**
A marketing agency was paying $500–$2,000 per blog post to freelance writers with 3–5 day turnaround. They needed to scale content production without burning their budget.

**Solution:**
I built an AI blog engine that researches topics from real web sources — reading published articles, extracting data and statistics — and generates complete, SEO-optimized blog posts. The engine produces YAML frontmatter, proper heading hierarchy, keyword targeting, and internal linking suggestions.

**Results:**
- 2-minute turnaround per post (from 3–5 days)
- $0.01 compute cost per post (from $500+ agency cost)
- 15+ SEO keywords extracted per post from real competitor content
- 8 real web sources researched per post — no AI hallucination
- Agency scaled from 4 posts/month to 30+ posts/month

**Technologies used:** Python, web search, content generation, SEO analysis, markdown publishing
