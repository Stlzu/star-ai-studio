# Cold Email Campaign Templates — AI Automation Services

---

## Campaign Strategy Overview

**Target:** 5 distinct verticals with tailored messaging
**Volume:** 10–20 personalized emails per day per vertical
**Sequence:** 3 emails per prospect, spaced 3 days apart
**Goal:** Book a 15-minute discovery call

### Tracking Approach

| Metric | Tool / Method |
|--------|---------------|
| Email opens | HubSpot Sales Hub (free tier) or MailTracker (Chrome extension) |
| Link clicks | Bit.ly or HubSpot link tracking |
| Replies | Manual — tag in CRM (HubSpot / Sheets) |
| Call bookings | Calendly link in every email |
| Follow-up triggers | Reply = move to nurture; Open but no reply = sequence continues; No open = resend with new subject |

**CRM setup:** Create a Google Sheet with columns: Company, Contact Name, Email, Vertical, Email 1 Sent, Opened, Replied, Email 2 Sent, Email 3 Sent, Call Booked, Notes.

**Send strategy:**
- Send between 7:30–9:00 AM local time (Tuesday–Thursday best)
- Personalize the first line of every email (reference a specific detail about their business)
- Never send more than 20 emails per domain per day to protect deliverability
- Use a warm-up tool (e.g., Mailwarm) for 2 weeks before cold campaigns

## Sequence Configuration Summary

| Day | Action | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| **Day 1 (Tue/Wed)** | Send Email 1 | Personalized first line, Calendly link |
| **Day 4 (Fri/Sat)** | Send Email 2 | Reference previous email, add value/detail |
| **Day 8 (Tue/Wed)** | Send Email 3 | Final follow-up, soft close, offer stands |

**After sequence:**
- **Replied:** Move to nurture — send case study, schedule call
- **Opened but no reply:** Wait 30 days, then re-enter sequence with new subject line
- **No open:** Try from a different sending address or LinkedIn DM

**Subject line A/B testing:**
- Test benefit-driven vs. curiosity-driven subjects
- Test with/without personalization brackets
- Track open rates per vertical — double down on what works

**Scaling:**
- Start with 1 vertical (accounting firms have highest ROI on invoice tool)
- Add 1 new vertical per week after sequences are running
- After 50+ sends per vertical, analyze reply rates and refine messaging
