## Discovery Call Script (30 min)

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks for jumping on. Quick intro — I build custom AI tools that automate repetitive business processes. My clients typically save 80%+ on time and costs for whatever we automate. To see if I can help you, I'd love to hear about what's taking up the most time in your day-to-day."

**Discovery Questions (15 min):**
1. "What's the most time-consuming repetitive task in your business right now?"
2. "How much time does that take per week?"
3. "Have you tried solving it with software before? What happened?"
4. "If I could automate that in a week, what would that be worth to you?"
5. "Are there any other processes you wish ran automatically?"

**Demo/Pitch (10 min):**
"Based on what you've shared, here's what I'd build for you..."
[Show relevant demo from our 3 products]

"I can have this running in 5 days. Here's the investment:
- Build: $X
- Monthly: $X
- First month free as a beta client"

**Close (3 min):**
"Does this sound like it'd solve your problem? Great — I'll send you a simple proposal by tomorrow, and if it looks good, I can start building next week."

## Objection Handling

**"This sounds expensive."**
"Fair question. Let me put it in perspective: if this saves you [X hours] per week at [Y dollars per hour], it pays for itself in [Z weeks]. Plus, you own the system — it's not a subscription service, it's an investment in efficiency."

**"I've tried AI before and it didn't work."**
"Most AI tools are generic. I build custom solutions for your specific process. Let me show you what I've built for [similar client] — it's not a chatbot, it's a fully automated workflow."

**"Can I see it working first?"**
"Absolutely. I'll build a quick demo with your actual data in 1-2 days. No obligation, no payment."

**"I need to think about it."**
"Totally understand. What questions do you still have? Is it the budget, the timeline, or the trust factor?"
